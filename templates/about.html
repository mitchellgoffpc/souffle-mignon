The Hitchhiker's Guide has this to say about Soufflé Mignon:
Soufflé Mignon is a blog run by Mitchell Goff and Gabby Moreno, both lifelong residents of the Sol system, since January of 2017. It is known mostly for its humor, its numerous xkcd references, and for being a huge waste of everyone's time.
Mitchell Goff
Mitchell is an omnivorous homo sapiens native to North America. He is most commonly found in the digital arts lab at Dartmouth College, where he studies Computer Science, although he migrates seasonally to the coastal areas of California as his budget allows. His favorite color is the shade of those pretty orange flecks in Gabby's eyes, but denim blue is a close second. In his spare time he likes making butterscotch cookies and playing Elton John songs over and over and over until everyone gets sick of them. On rare occasions he can be spotted in the wild, where his preferred activities include rock climbing, mountain biking, and kayaking. He is currently 21 years old.
Gabriela Moreno
¡Gabriela Moreno! is her name when her parents are mad about something. She is 20 years old. She studies Cognitive Science and Computational Linguistics & Computer Science at the University of Southern California. Try saying that five times fast without sounding pretentious. Doesn't like it when things are pronounced incorrectly yet will go out of her way to pronounce things in a subtly annoying way like saying /suh-poh-sub-ly/ instead of supposedly. Had perfect attendance since pre-school. No, seriously. Her favourite color is blue but not like a light blue, it has to be like a midnight blue. Part time Question Asker, full-time professional Finder Outer. She resides in Los Angeles. Se habla español.
